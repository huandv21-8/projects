Zoo rules:

1. Entrance to the zoo is only possible with a valid ticket.

2. Dog entrance is allowed for a person over 15 years old after a valid tick purchase. A dog must be worn muzzle and chained on a visit.

3. Access for children under 10 years old is allowed only for one person over 15 years old.

4. A leader of the mass tour is responsible for the safety of the group. For the safety of children is the responsibility of adults.

5. Follow the instructions of the Zoo staff.


Zoos are prohibited

1. Do not disrupt animals in any form.

2. Do not arbitrarily feed strange foods not provided by the Kawazaki Zoo.

3. Do not bring digital audio / noise devices, guns (water guns, air guns, paint guns, etc.) and other weapons to the zoo.

4. Do not climb, cross walls, median strips specified in Zoo.

Some things to notice

- Please, follow all zoo zoo staff instructions and information boards!

- After a zoo rule violation, zoo staff can call the police, who will remove the guest from the zoo.

- Zoo is not responsible for any loss or injury of visitors, whose origin is related to violation of zoo rules.
