    Otus lettia

    Otus lettia is a species of bird in the Strigidae family

    Describe

    23cm. The coat is dark brown. The eyes are brown (rarely yellow), which is different from the small owl. Young birds have dark brown stripes. Only works at night. When the call is parked high above and can be detected by flashlight light. This breed is usually towards the light and is not afraid of people. The call: ‘hu-o’ sounds soothing and sobbing, repeating each occasion about 10 seconds apart.

    Current Status

    Settlement species. Popular.

    Food

    Their food is mainly rats, birds and insects

    Living Habitat

    Forests, secondary forests and village areas. Breed in around February-August. Lay 2-5 eggs. They often nest in tree cavities and are distributed throughout the country

    Tyto alba

    Tyto alba is the most widely distributed species of the Pig Owl family. Tên khoa học là Tyto alba.

    Distribution & Accommodation

    Distributed throughout the world, except in polar regions and deserts, in Asia north of the Alpide belt, much of Indonesia, and the Pacific Islands. In Vietnam, this species is often found in urban areas, nesting on abandoned roofs or on high walls in the forest, where there are large trees, abundant food sources, in cities and temples. Build a nest there, meet on the wall, the roof. Lay 4-7 eggs.

    Describe

    34cm. Heart-shaped disc. The belly is white or dark brown. The back is gray with reddish brown stripes. The face is dark reddish brown with dark brown horizontal stripes. The beak is covered with feathers only to reveal the tip of the beak; thighs and shin covered with fur.

    Glaucidium cuculoides

    Glaucidium cuculoides is a species of bird in the Strigidae family

    Describe

    26cm. Cat owls do not have ears, a round face, and are more horizontal in position than other small owls. The back is brown with a checkered pattern, the dark brown tail looks like an additional attachment. Yellow eyes. Hunt during the day. Screams: 'Plump' in the daytime, like the sound made when a bottle full of water is tilted upside down.
