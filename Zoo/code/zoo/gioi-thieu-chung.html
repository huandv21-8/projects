    Introduce


    Kawazaki Zoo is one of the largest zoos in the city. The zoo's land area is 33 acres. Kawazaki Zoo was founded in 1980 with 10 acres of land but recently it was modified and the land area was also added in 2000. Suitable residence spaces for all types of animals have been created and maintain the security of human at high speed.


    Kawazaki Zoo is the first open zoo model in Vietnam. The animals live in an open space, separated from Tourists by rivers and streams, creating a friendly feeling between people and nature. With a diverse collection of animals, the zoo currently nourishes and conserves 63 mammal species with more than 600 individuals. In particular, the Kawazaki Zoo has succeeded in breeding rare animals, in danger of extinction such as: Indochinese Tiger, Nai Ca-Tong, Voc ...

    In addition, the zoo display space at Kawazaki Zoo is not only a monotonous animal but also a collection of species with similar living conditions. Each of which has its own characteristics but has a mutual impact.




    With open space design, visitors can approach and interact with residents zoo friendly through activities such as feeding giraffes, taking photos with antelopes, playing with apes ... You also have access to and observe the life of wild and rare animals; At the same time, witness the animals raised and reproduced in the semi-wild ecological environment


    More specifically, at Kawazaki Zoo, you have the opportunity to observe the rare animals that first appeared Vietnam as: White Lion, White Tiger, Antelope Horned Horn, South American Squirrel Monkey ....
