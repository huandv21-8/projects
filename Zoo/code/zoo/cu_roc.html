    Psilopogon asiaticus

    Psilopogon asiaticus is a species of bird in the Megalaimidae family

    Adult bird

    The forehead, the front of the neck and the two points on both sides of the throat are bright red. A wide band in the middle of the top of the head before the eyes, cheeks, ears, chin, and pale throat. The rest of the fur is green, the back is slightly darker, the belly is lighter and slightly yellow. Pale black wing feathers, green outer edge, inner white edge. The under eye of tail tail is greenish. Brown eyes. The skin around the eyes is pale yellow. The beak is black or dark brown, the base is hazel white. Pale gray legs.

    Size

    Wings: 92 - 110; tail: 62 - 70; beak: 23 - 27 mm.

    Distribution

    Vietnam: This species is found in almost all areas, where there are many dense trees and not more than 800 m high. It seems that the southern border of the red-headed barbet is Quang Binh

    Megalaima lineata

    Megalaima lineata is a species of bird in the Megalaimidae family

    Adult Bird

    Pale brown head and neck, each hair more or less pale white and looks like a bar. Full back face green. Pale white chin: throat, chest and upper part are light golden-brown, each hair has a broad white line along the body. The back of the abdomen, ribs and under the tail feel green. Brown eyes. Beak and bare skin around golden eyes. Golden foot.

              

    Size

    Wings: 117 - 139; tail: 84; Mine: 31mm.

    Distribution

    Megalaima lineata distributed in the Himalayas, Burma, Thailand and Indochina. Vietnam can meet this species everywhere from Quang Tri to the South such as Mount Dinh Ba Ria - Vung Tau, Chua Chan Mountain, Xuan Loc - Dong Nai.
